# Asset Design Document for Chapter 1

## Backgrounds

1. **[Background] bg plaza_day**
   - **用途**：斗气测试广场，白天，人声鼎沸。
   - **风格**：东方玄幻风格，宽阔的石板广场，中央竖立着巨大的黑色石碑（测验魔石碑），周围有古朴的建筑和树木，阳光明媚。
   - **搜索关键词**：fantasy plaza, stone tablet, crowd, ancient chinese architecture, sunny day.
   - **AI提示词**：Wide shot of an ancient fantasy plaza, massive black stone monolith in the center with glowing runes, crowded with people in traditional robes, sunny sky, detailed stone texture, cinematic lighting, anime style background.

2. **[Background] bg plaza_dusk**
   - **用途**：测试结束，夕阳西下，两人对话。
   - **风格**：同上，但光线变为暖橙色的夕阳，影子拉长，氛围变得宁静而略带忧伤。
   - **搜索关键词**：fantasy plaza sunset, emotional atmosphere, long shadows.
   - **AI提示词**：Ancient fantasy plaza at sunset, warm orange lighting, long shadows, empty or few people, melancholic atmosphere, sentimental mood, detailed clouds, anime style background.

## Music (BGM)

1. **[BGM] sad_theme**
   - **用途**：萧炎测试失败，遭受嘲笑。
   - **风格**：悲伤，压抑，低沉，钢琴或大提琴独奏。
   - **听觉描述**：Slow piano melody, melancholic cello, low notes, sad and depressive atmosphere, minimal orchestration.

2. **[BGM] lively_theme**
   - **用途**：萧媚出场，展示天赋。
   - **风格**：轻快，活泼，明亮，带有一点虚荣感的弦乐。
   - **听觉描述**：Upbeat strings, flute, bright and cheerful, youthful energy, slightly pompous.

3. **[BGM] elegant_theme**
   - **用途**：萧薰儿出场，震撼全场。
   - **风格**：高贵，神秘，优雅，空灵，古琴或竖琴。
   - **听觉描述**：Ethereal harp, guqin, elegant and mysterious, high class, awe-inspiring, serene.

4. **[BGM] warm_theme**
   - **用途**：萧薰儿安慰萧炎。
   - **风格**：温暖，治愈，柔和，带有一丝酸楚的钢琴与弦乐合奏。
   - **听觉描述**：Warm piano, gentle strings, sentimental, healing, bittersweet, romantic undertone.

## Sound Effects (SFX)

1. **[SFX] crowd_mockery**
   - **用途**：人群嘲笑萧炎。
   - **听觉描述**：Crowd laughing, jeering, whispering, murmuring, negative reaction.

2. **[SFX] crowd_praise**
   - **用途**：人群赞叹萧媚。
   - **听觉描述**：Crowd cheering, clapping, admiring gasps, "wow" sounds.

3. **[SFX] silence_then_murmur**
   - **用途**：萧薰儿测试结果公布后的反应。
   - **听觉描述**：Sudden silence, followed by a wave of low murmurs and gasps of shock.
