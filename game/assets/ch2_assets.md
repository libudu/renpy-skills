# Asset Design Document for Chapter 2

## Backgrounds

1. **[Background] bg cliff_night**
   - **用途**：后山悬崖，萧炎独处，父子对话。
   - **风格**：宁静，神秘，巨大的满月，星空，悬崖边有草地和树林。
   - **搜索关键词**：cliff night, full moon, starry sky, fantasy landscape, peaceful night.
   - **AI 提示词**：Night scene, edge of a cliff, giant silver full moon, starry sky, grassy field, silhouette of trees, peaceful atmosphere, anime style background, 4k, detailed.

## Music (BGM)

1. **[BGM] night_theme**

   - **用途**：开场，萧炎独处。
   - **风格**：宁静，空灵，带有一点孤独感，虫鸣背景声。
   - **听觉描述**：Ambient night sounds, crickets, soft wind, minimal piano or flute, peaceful but lonely.

2. **[BGM] angry_theme**

   - **用途**：萧炎回忆穿越，愤怒咆哮。
   - **风格**：激昂，悲愤，节奏加快，甚至带有一点摇滚或管弦乐的爆发。
   - **听觉描述**：Sudden tempo increase, dramatic strings, heavy percussion, angry and frustrated mood, cinematic climax.

3. **[BGM] father_theme**
   - **用途**：萧战出场，父子对话。
   - **风格**：温暖，厚重，大提琴或低音提琴，体现父爱和家族责任。
   - **听觉描述**：Warm cello, orchestral, slow tempo, comforting, emotional, slightly heavy due to the burden of the family.

## Visual Effects (VFX) / Items

1. **[Item] ring_black**

   - **用途**：萧炎手上的戒指，特写。
   - **风格**：古朴，黑色，材质不明，有模糊的纹路。
   - **AI 提示词**：Close up of a hand wearing an ancient black ring, mysterious material, engraved with faint runes, dark and weathered, realistic texture.

2. **[VFX] ring_glow**
   - **用途**：结尾戒指发光。
   - **描述**：微弱的毫光，一闪而逝。
   - **实现建议**：Ren'Py transform using `alpha` or an overlay image.
